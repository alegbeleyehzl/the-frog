TOOLS

Forceps

Instruments that are used to grip and hold items. It is a set of essential tools that are commonly used in laboratory and clinical settings. Scalpel blades come in a variety of sizes, each of which serves a particular purpose, and have been used "for performing skin incisions, tissue dissections, and a variety of surgical methods since the beginning of 'modern' surgery."
