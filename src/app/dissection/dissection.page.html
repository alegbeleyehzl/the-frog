VIRTUAL DISSECTION

To start the virtual dissection, click the plus (+) button beside the frog. Click it each time the tools disappear. The tools attached are the PIN (📌), SCALPEL (🔪), and SCISSOR (✂️).


INSTRUCTIONS:
  1. Click the PIN (📌) to pinch the frog.
  2. Click the SCALPEL (🔪) to make a line.
  3. Once again, click the PIN (📌) to open new layer.
  4. Click the Scissor (✂️) to open the abdominal part of the frog.
  5. Click the PIN (📌).
  6. Lastly, click the word "NEXT" that will appear on the screen.

TOOLS

Forceps

Instruments that are used to grip and hold items. It is a set of essential tools that are commonly used in laboratory and clinical settings. They're made of steel or nylon, and the tips, or ends, come in a variety of shapes and sizes, including straight, curved, blunt, rat tooth, flat, and fine pointed.

Scalpel

Scalpel blades come in a variety of sizes, each of which serves a particular purpose, and have been used "for performing skin incisions, tissue dissections, and a variety of surgical methods since the beginning of 'modern' surgery."

Scissors

It is use for cutting tissues.

Needle

Needles are used to dissect, separate, and manipulate tissue without tearing it.

Magnifying Glass

Used for viewing materials up close with more details.

Dissecting Spatula

The dissecting spatula is used for extraction. It may also be used to keep tissues from recovering (as in keeping out of sight again) your subject.
